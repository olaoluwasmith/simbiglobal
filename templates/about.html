Simbi Global Resources Limited is a private company by shares, incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 by the Corporate Affairs Commission, Abuja on 3rd June, 2014.

OUR MISSION

  • To engage in using sustainable agricultural methods (traditional and modern) for the production of healthy food at affordable prices through integrated extension activities.
  • To have the competencies for providing seamless and cost effective logistics solutions; intelligently and accurately within the time bound.
  • We take pride in consistently delivering quality results for all merchandising activities and property development.

OUR VISION

  • Focusing on resource synergies to create a niche and have an edge in the market, both in value, consistency and quality of services, products and properties.
  • Continually upgrading the standards of operations and processes; improving on innovative ways of doing things as well as improving the quality of properties, products and services with the shortest period of time possible and therby become a recognized brand.

OUR BELIEVE

  • Creating jobs for our teaming youths.
  • Our workforce and customers are our valuable assets.
  • A business environment where great innovative ideas are encouraged and supported.
  • In integrity, commitment and dedication to our course with every sense of responsibility.
  • In being a critical player involved in stimulating economic growth.
  • Harmonization of resources for value creation.
